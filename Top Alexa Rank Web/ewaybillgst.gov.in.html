ATTENTION TAX PAYERS
An alert message will now appear, while generating e-Way bill,either as consignor or consignee,with respect to those taxpayers who have not filed
GSTR 3B return, for last two consecutive months!
However users can proceed further to generate e-Way bill.
If default in return filing continues, the facility to generate e-Way bill, for such GSTIN’s (either as consignor or consignee), will be stopped in due course.

In case, you have filed the Return, but system shows you have not filed , then you may update the status by clicking on the following link :
https://ewaybillgst.gov.in -> Search --> ‘Update Block Status’.
